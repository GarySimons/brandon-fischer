BIOGRAPHY

Born and raised in Frankfurt, Brandon majored in photography, film and video from The Hochschule für Bildende Künste Dresden. His degree course work included painting, drawing and sculpture.

Brandon has been placed as a finalist in the European Architectural Photography Prize Architekturbild, in 2014, and his work has been shown at photo festivals around the world.

Since year 2004, Brandon has been lecturing on architectural photography at UUniversität der Künste Berlin for graduates taking master degrees in interior design. The course analyses a wide range of architectural photographs and discusses their use as communication tools in books,magazines and blogs.

Brandon lives in Berlin’s lively Neukölln district.
